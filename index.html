Kleju(Poland)

Bboy Kleju

  • passion:breaking
  • current location:Krakow, Poland
  • TL Clan member since:2014
  • Proper name: Tomasz Kuliś
  • Born:1989

One of the world’s most recognizable bboys, Kleju comes straight out of Krakow, Poland. Besides his affro and sun glasses that he rocks during his battles, musicality is usually what gets him remembered. Kleju is part of the Polskee Flavour and Funky Masons crew and he is one of the co-founders for Catch the Flava camp. On the side, he is amazing video editor, musician (violin, guitar), and singer.
